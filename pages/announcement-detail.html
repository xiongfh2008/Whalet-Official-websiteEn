Qingming Festival Holiday Notice

2025-03-21 17:54:59

Dear Whalet Users:

Due to the Qingming Festival holiday (April 4-6, 2025), the processing times for various services will be adjusted as follows. Please plan your finances accordingly.

1. Payment Receipt

Delays in payment processing for Mainland China/Hong Kong/Indonesia due to holiday

2. Withdrawals and Payments

Withdrawal and payment services will be suspended and resume on April 7

3. Whalet Card Services

Whalet Card balance transfers will resume on April 7

4. Other Services

Account verification and store/order review will be suspended and processed in order on April 7

We apologize for any inconvenience. For any questions, please contact your dedicated sales manager or our customer service at 400-806-0877.
