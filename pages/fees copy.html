Fee Information

Fee Calculation

Percentage fees are calculated based on transaction amount, while fixed fees are constant. Some transactions may have both percentage and fixed fees.

Preferential Policies

Different fee discounts are available based on your membership level and monthly transaction volume. Please consult your account manager for details.

Other Fees

Additional fees may apply for special transactions such as cross-border transfers and currency exchange. Please check the detailed fee schedule before proceeding.

Important Notice

Fees are subject to change. Please refer to the actual fees displayed in the system at the time of transaction. Contact customer support if you have any questions.
