Upgrade Notice

  • Personal accounts can be upgraded to Mainland China enterprise accounts or Hong Kong enterprise accounts. The relationship between personal users and enterprises: Mainland China Account: Personal user must be the legal representative, actual controller, or shareholder with more than 25% ownership
    Hong Kong Enterprise: Personal user must be a director, actual controller, or shareholder with more than 25% ownership
  • The Mainland China enterprise or Hong Kong enterprise to be upgraded has not registered a Whalet account
  • Account upgrade operation is irreversible, and you cannot return to the original personal account after upgrading
  • After account upgrade, all existing payment accounts, card issuance limits, payment records, and withdrawal records will be retained and unaffected

Please prepare the following document photos

  • Business License Copy
  • Identity Documents of Key Personnel Description: ID card front/back and holding ID photo of legal representative, company directors, actual controllers, and shareholders with 25% or more ownership (Mainland) or passport and holding passport photo
  • Holding Account Opening Application Form Please fill out the Account Opening Application Form (Click to Download) and stamp with company seal, provide photo of legal representative/director holding the application form
